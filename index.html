Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer emergency care services?

Yes, we have an on-call emergency team available 24/7. However, for severe life-threatening trauma, we recommend visiting the main city animal hospital while calling us en route.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a 24-hour notice for appointment cancellations. Cancellations within 24 hours will incur a 50% consultation fee to respect our specialists' scheduled time.

Do you accept pet insurance?

Absolutely. We collaborate with all major pet insurance providers. We can assist you with submitting claims, though payments are required upfront at the time of service.

How do I transition my pet to your recommended raw/fresh diet?

Dietary transitions should be slow, typically over 7-10 days, gradually mixing the new food with the old. Our nutritionists will provide a personalized step-by-step guide after your consultation.
